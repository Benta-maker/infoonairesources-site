About Info on AI Resources

Info on AI Resources is a trusted hub for AI knowledge, tools, and governance, curated with a focus on data protection, ethics, and accessibility. Our mission is to empower businesses, educators, and individuals to harness artificial intelligence responsibly while navigating evolving legal and regulatory landscapes.

Mission

We strive to provide safe, compliant and actionable AI resources that enable innovation without compromising privacy or security.

Vision

To be the go-to platform bridging AI knowledge and governance, fostering a community that champions responsible AI for Africa and beyond.

Values

  • Safety: We prioritize user safety and regulatory compliance in every resource we share.
  • Inclusivity: We ensure accessibility and consider diverse cultural contexts, especially across the African continent.
  • Transparency: We promote open dialogue about AI’s impacts, benefits, and risks.
